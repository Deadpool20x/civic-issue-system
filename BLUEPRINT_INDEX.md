## 📊 System Overview

**Project:** Civic Issue Management System
**Purpose:** Platform for citizens to report civic issues and track resolution
**Users:** Citizens, Department Staff, Municipal Officers, Administrators
**Tech:** Next.js 14, MongoDB, JWT, Cloudinary, OpenAI, Resend, Leaflet

**Key Features:**
- Issue reporting with AI categorization
- Auto-assignment to departments
- SLA tracking and escalation
- Performance metrics
- Email notifications
- Interactive maps
- Rating and feedback

**Database:** 7 collections, 50+ API endpoints, 30+ pages
**Estimated Rebuild Time:** 15 days
**Skill Level:** Intermediate to Advanced Full-Stack
